2013-02-02
San Mateo County Office of Education, San Mateo, CA
 Council of Math/Science Educators of San Mateo County (CMSESMC) Conference
http://cmsesmc.org/ The 37th Annual STEM Conference provides many sessions that foster excellence in math, science and technology curricula, educational programs, and promotes professional excellence in teaching and strengthening leadership in these fields. CMSESMC is the acronym (and a palindrome) for the Council of Math/Science Educators of San Mateo County. This professional grass-roots organization of educators and others interested in math, science and technology education, pre-K through college, represents both public and private institutions within San Mateo County and the greater Bay Area.
